Project

Root Theory is a botanical haircare brand system built around the belief that healthy hair begins at the root. The identity combines herbal care, ritual-based routines, and a tactile seal-mark language.

Details

  • Scope: Brand identity, typography, stationery, posters
  • Format: Brand brief and campaign system
  • Assets: 5 brand slides, 5 stationery pieces, 5 poster applications
